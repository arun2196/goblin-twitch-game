# Commands

## !chest

Open a treasure chest.

Rewards:

- Gold
- Random item

Inventory limit:

- 3 items

---

## !delve

Explore a random delve.

Possible outcomes:

- Success
- Failure

Gold may be gained or lost.

---

## !challenge @user amount

Challenge another goblin.

Requirements:

- Both players have gold
- Both players have at least one item

---

## !ready

Accept duel.

---

## !run

Decline duel.

---

## !askgobbo question

Ask the Goblin Oracle a question.

Example:

!askgobbo should I trust chickens?
